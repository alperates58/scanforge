# Ethical and Legal Policy

ScanForge is for assets owned by the user or explicitly authorized for testing.

## Required Controls
- Domain verification before active scans
- Audit logs
- Rate limits
- Safe defaults
- No destructive tests
- No credential attacks
- Clear scan consent checkbox

## UI Consent Text
I confirm that I own this website or have explicit authorization to scan it. I understand that even safe automated scans create traffic and may be logged by the target hosting provider.
