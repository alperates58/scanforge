# Phase 09 - SSL, DNS, Headers, Cookies

## Goal
Premium sağlık kontrolleri.

## Checks
- TLS version
- certificate expiry
- HSTS
- CSP
- X-Frame-Options
- X-Content-Type-Options
- Referrer-Policy
- Permissions-Policy
- Cookie Secure/HttpOnly/SameSite
- SPF/DKIM/DMARC

## Acceptance
- Skor ve öneriler üretilir.
